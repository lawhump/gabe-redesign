Gabe Diaz-Barriga > About > Full Bio

Gabe Diaz-Barriga was born in Mobile, Alabama and grew up in Tennessee.

Gabe got his start in design through drawing as a kid. In high school, his interests morphed into graphic design and photography – he was predominantly self-taught. In college, his passion for design grew wider and deeper. In his business courses at Babson College he was introduced to “design thinking” as a method for creative problem-solving. He then cross-registered at neighboring Olin College where he studied and practiced design intensely. In his final semester at Babson he planned and instructed a half-semester course called By Design. In August of 2016 he will begin his career as a Product Designer at Facebook.

Tidbits – Little Details Matter

tidbits.love
2016
With Jared Silver

Tidbits is a small app that helps you remember little things about the people who matter to you.

Tidbits was inspired by the realization that forgetting details about someone does not mean that you do not care about them, but remembering those things is a simple and powerful way to show that you do.

Tidbits is great for: meeting new people, starting school, joining a team, professional networking events, moving to a new city, or running a small business.
